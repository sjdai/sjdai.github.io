Research Experience

Jul 2017 ~ Aug. 2021

Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis Lab, Academia Sinica

Advisor:Dr. Lun-Wei Ku

I am primarily working on NLP applications. Currently, I am focusing on mitigating the influence of misinformation using NLP, and I have some publications related to the research. First, we constructed a recommendation system leveraging reinforcement learning for guiding the user who read the fake news to read the corresponding verified news article. Second, we built a framework to generate the explanation for why a piece of fake news was detected as fake news. There are three steps to the process. First, we generating a couple of question-answering pairs from the fake claims and evidence documents using Spacy and T5 Transformer. Second, we trained an entailment model using RoBerta to pick up the best question-answering pair. Finally, we generated the explanation using BART.
